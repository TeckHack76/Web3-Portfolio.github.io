Work

I recently undertook the development of my personal portfolio website to showcase my skills, projects, and experiences in the field of programming and web development. The project aimed to provide a professional online presence and serve as a central hub for anyone interested in my WORK.

I utilized an HTML template from html5up as the foundation for my portfolio. The template provided a clean and responsive design, saving time and ensuring a modern and visually appealing layout.

I extensively edited the HTML and CSS code to personalize the template according to my preferences. This included adjusting the color scheme, modifying layout elements, and ensuring overall coherence with my branding.

I added relevant content, including sections for my skills, projects, education, and contact information. Each section was crafted to provide a comprehensive overview of my capabilities and experiences.

To enhance visual appeal, I incorporated images related to my projects, education, and personal background. These images were carefully selected to complement the content and create a visually engaging portfolio.

I included links to my github repository, allowing visitors to explore my code and projects in more detail. This integration provided transparency and showcased my commitment to open-source collaboration.
